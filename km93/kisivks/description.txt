1. Sample_duration - The length of time that air passes through the monitoring device before it is analyzed (measured).
2. Date_local - The calendar date for the summary. All daily summaries are for the local standard day
(midnight to midnight) at the monitor.
3. Units_of_measure - The unit of measure for the parameter. QAD always returns data in the standard units for the
parameter.
4. County_name - The name of the county where the monitoring site is located.
